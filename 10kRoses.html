A Beatiful Place to Relax: 10k Roses



If you're looking for a place to relax near a coast, then 10k Roses is the place for you. A cafe that is in a perfect spot for relaxation and planespotting.

10000 Roses a cafe with it's main attraction the White Roses that lights up in the night, located at the end of Cordova Tourism Center, just right next to Lantaw Floating Restaurant.

With it's popularity that's been a talk since it's opening in 2017, my expections are high. Though it is indeed a beatiful sight, that's all there is about it. Roses that lights up at night. But I do like the atmosphere there, overall it was not that great, but a decent experience seeing it for my self.
